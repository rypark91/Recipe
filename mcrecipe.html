Ryan's Pure Macaroni and Cheese

What you need(serves 1 person)

  • 1 cup of Water
  • 1 cup of Macaroni (or pasta shells)
  • 1 cup of Grated White Cheddar Cheese
  • 1 table spoon of butter.
  • 1/4 cup of Heavy Cream (or half and half if you don't want the extra calories).

How to actually make it.

  1. boil water on high.
  2. Put pasta in water on while switching to medium high until pasta is soft.
  3. Drain pasta
  4. Place Cream and Butter in the pan with the pasta
  5. Turn heat on low, then place cheese in pan to melt.
  6. Stir until cheese is melted.
  7. Dig in!!!
